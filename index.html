Frequently asked questions
What's the difference between Avalanche and Snowball?
Avalanche pays the highest-interest debt first — minimizing total interest paid. Snowball pays the smallest balance first — delivering faster wins to keep you motivated. Avalanche almost always saves more money; Snowball is better if you need encouragement.
Is my data saved anywhere?
No. DebtZero runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server, no cookies are set, no account is created. Your financial data never leaves your device.
How accurate are the calculations?
DebtZero uses standard monthly compounding (APR ÷ 12). Real lenders may use daily compounding, so treat results as a close estimate. Consult your actual statements for exact figures.
What does "What if?" show me?
Four instant scenarios: doubling your extra payment, refinancing all debts to 5% APR, skipping one month's extra payment, and the monthly amount needed to clear everything in 12 months.
Which currency should I use?
Select your local currency from the dropdown — it changes number formatting and the symbol throughout. The calculations are currency-agnostic; only the display changes.
Can I share my results?
You can take a screenshot or print the page. Because there's no server, there's no shareable URL — that's intentional, to protect your privacy.
